About the CSE department

The Computer Systems Engineering (CSE) Department at Quaid-e-Awam University of Engineering, Sciences & Technology (QUEST), Nawabshah, offers a dynamic Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) program. The department integrates computer science with electrical engineering, focusing on essential areas like computer architecture, embedded systems, networking, and cybersecurity.
